🌟 Highlights

What Makes Jaipur Special

🏰

Amber Fort

Majestic hilltop fortress with stunning architecture, mirror palace, and panoramic views. Take an elephant ride to the entrance.

🪟

Hawa Mahal

The iconic "Palace of Winds" with 953 windows. A masterpiece of Rajput architecture and Jaipur's most photographed landmark.

🛍️

Vibrant Bazaars

Johari Bazaar for jewelry, Bapu Bazaar for textiles, and Tripolia Bazaar for lacquer bangles. A shopper's paradise.

🍛

Royal Cuisine

Dal Baati Churma, Laal Maas, Ghewar, and Pyaaz Kachori. Taste the authentic flavors of Rajasthani royalty.

🌅

Nahargarh Fort

Stunning sunset point overlooking the Pink City. The fort offers breathtaking panoramic views and a rooftop restaurant.

📐

Jantar Mantar

UNESCO World Heritage astronomical observatory. The world's largest stone sundial and other incredible scientific instruments.
