🌟 Highlights

What Makes Odisha Special

🛕

Jagannath Temple

The iconic 12th-century temple in Puri dedicated to Lord Jagannath, one of the four sacred dhams in India. The annual Rath Yatra chariot festival draws millions of devotees from around the world.

☀️

Konark Sun Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site — a colossal 13th-century temple shaped like a giant chariot with intricately carved stone wheels. One of the most breathtaking architectural feats in human history.

🏖️

Puri Beach

One of India's most famous beaches, stretching along the Bay of Bengal. Witness spectacular sunrises, the famous Puri beach festival, and enjoy the soothing sound of ocean waves.

🦢

Chilika Lake

Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, home to Irrawaddy dolphins, flamingos, and migratory birds from Siberia. A paradise for birdwatchers and nature lovers.

🎨

Pattachitra Art

Odisha's ancient cloth-based scroll painting tradition depicting mythological stories. Vibrant, detailed, and deeply rooted in the culture of Puri — a must-buy souvenir.

🌿

Tribal Culture

Odisha is home to over 60 indigenous tribal communities. Visit the Koraput region to experience authentic tribal markets, dances, crafts, and a way of life untouched by time.
