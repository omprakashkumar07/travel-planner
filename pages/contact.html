❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TravelPlanner free to use?+

Yes! TravelPlanner is completely free. You can explore destinations, plan budgets, and chat with our AI assistant at no cost.

How accurate are the budget estimates?+

Our budget estimates are based on real traveler data and are regularly updated. They provide a good baseline, though actual costs may vary by season and personal preferences.

Can I save my travel plans?+

Yes! Your selected destination, budget, and preferences are saved locally in your browser. You can revisit the budget planner or chat anytime to continue planning.

Do you cover international destinations?+

Currently, TravelPlanner focuses on India's top destinations. We're working on adding international locations — stay tuned!
