🌟 Highlights

What Makes Manali Special

🏔️

Rohtang Pass

Drive through the stunning Rohtang Pass at 13,050 ft. Snow-capped peaks, glaciers, and breathtaking panoramic views await.

🪂

Adventure Sports

Paragliding in Solang Valley, river rafting in Beas, zorbing, and skiing in winter. An adrenaline junkie's paradise.

🌲

Old Manali

Charming hippie village with cozy cafes, bakeries, live music, and a laid-back vibe. Walk the cobbled lanes and unwind.

🛕

Hadimba Temple

Ancient wooden temple nestled in a cedar forest. A masterpiece of architecture surrounded by tall deodar trees.

🥾

Trekking Trails

Jogini Waterfalls, Beas Kund, Hampta Pass — trails for every level from easy walks to challenging multi-day treks.

☕

Cafe Culture

Sip hot chocolate at mountain cafes with stunning valley views. Cafe 1947, Lazy Dog, and Drifters Inn are must-visits.
