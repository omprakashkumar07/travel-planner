🌟 Highlights

What Makes Kerala Special

🛶

Alleppey Backwaters

Cruise through serene palm-lined canals on a traditional houseboat. Watch life unfold along the waterways — a magical experience.

☕

Munnar Tea Gardens

Rolling carpets of emerald green tea plantations. Visit the Tea Museum, trek through the hills, and breathe fresh mountain air.

🐘

Periyar Wildlife

Spot wild elephants, tigers, and exotic birds in Periyar National Park. Bamboo rafting and nature walks through dense forests.

🏖️

Kovalam & Varkala

Crescent-shaped beaches with dramatic cliff-top views. Perfect for surfing, yoga, and watching spectacular sunsets.

🍛

Kerala Cuisine

Malabar Biryani, Fish Moilee, Appam with Stew, Kerala Sadya served on banana leaf. A food lover's dream destination.

🧘

Ayurvedic Wellness

Kerala is the birthplace of Ayurveda. Rejuvenate with traditional treatments, yoga retreats, and holistic healing centers.
